About PLEX SUB

Getting Started

Welcome to PLEX SUB! Our subscription service provides you with exclusive access to our Plex server. In this guide, you'll find instructions on how to get started and make the most of your subscription.

Step 1: Sign Up

Visit our home page and subscribe by entering your email address. Once you sign up, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. This email will be used to send you the Plex invite.

Step 2: Payment

After signing up, you'll be redirected to our secure payment page powered by Stripe. Enter your payment details to start your monthly subscription. We take security seriously, ensuring your information is processed safely.

Step 3: Receive Your Plex Invite

Upon successful payment, our system will automatically send a Plex invite to your email. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new Plex account or log into your existing account. This invite will grant you access to our media server.

Step 4: Enjoy Your Content

Once you have accepted the Plex invite, you can start enjoying our extensive media library. Stream your favorite movies, TV shows, and more at your leisure.
